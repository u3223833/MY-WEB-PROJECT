Who Was Robin Boyd?

Robin Boyd was an influential Australian architect known for his distinctive modernist designs. He advocated for functional, simplified architecture and wrote extensively on the evolution of Australian building styles.

Learn more about his life, philosophy, and iconic projects throughout this site.

Design Philosophy

Boyd believed that architecture should be accessible, practical, and visually clean. He emphasized the use of space and materials to reflect a modern, straightforward design.
